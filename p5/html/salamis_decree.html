2. Context

Title: Salamis Decree

Find Spot: Acropolis

Location: Epigraphical Museum, inv. EM 6798+6815+12936, AM 7021

Description: Seven marble fragments, five of them conjoined, of a horizontally tapered stele. The text is inscribed on the horizontal face. Min. H. 0.219m. Min. L. 0.976m.

Letter Forms: Archaic letters, including tailed rho, three-bar sigma. Stoichedon on ll. 1-6.

Images: Kirchner, IIA.2,13; Austin pl.4; Schweigert, Hesp. 7 (1938) 265 (new frag.); Meritt, Hesp. 10 (1941) 305; Matthaiou Horos 8-9 (1990-91) [1993] 11 (facsimile); CSAD http://www.csad.ox.ac.uk/CSAD/Images/00/Image07.html

Date: ca. 510-500 BCE (Raubitschek, DAA, suggests 490-480 BCE)
